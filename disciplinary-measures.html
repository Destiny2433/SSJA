Disciplinary Measures

Guiding students toward responsible behavior with fairness and consistency.

Our Approach

  • Correction with respect: Disciplinary action aims to teach and reform.
  • Fairness: Students are treated consistently and with due consideration.
  • Progressive steps: Actions increase in seriousness only when necessary.
  • Restorative focus: Where possible, we encourage reflection and improvement.

Common Measures

Verbal warning
Written warning
Parent/guardian meeting
Detention / extra duties
Suspension (for serious cases)

Important Notes

Student Safety

Discipline ensures a safe learning environment for everyone.

Respect for Procedures

Cases are handled through school guidelines and communication with parents.
