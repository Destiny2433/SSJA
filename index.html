Hello There!!!

Welcome to SS Joachim And Anne School

You are welcome to SS. Joachim And Anne. SS. Joachim And Anne is a Catholic school providing a nurturing environment where faith, academic excellence, and personal growth converge School with the vision and mission of a balanced education for every child. The school operates an educational system based on the current national policy of education. Our school is located in the serene environment of Gowon Estate, Egbeda, Lagos. We also have state-of-the-art facilities to aid and enhance your child’s development.

SS. Joachim And Anne School is the right school for your child.
