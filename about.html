About Us

Our History

Founded in 2014 by Rev. Fr Dan Monagan and later transferred to Rev. Fr. Ernest, Sts. Joachim and Anne Catholic School has been a beacon of Catholic education in Egbeda, Lagos, Nigeria. We are committed to providing a holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit.
