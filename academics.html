Our Academic Structure

We offer a comprehensive Catholic education from Nursery to Secondary levels, integrating faith-based learning with a rigorous academic curriculum designed to prepare students for global challenges.

Academic Levels

Nursery

Ages 2-5: Play-based learning with emphasis on social, emotional, and spiritual development.

Primary

Ages 6-11: Foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and Catholic doctrine.

Secondary

Ages 12-18: Advanced curriculum preparing for WAEC, NECO, and university entrance.

Curriculum Highlights

Religious Education

Daily prayers, Mass attendance, and scripture studies to foster spiritual growth.

STEM & Arts

Integrated science, technology, arts, and humanities for well-rounded development.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports, music, debate, and community service to build leadership skills.

Assessment & Support

Continuous assessment, counseling, and personalized learning plans.
